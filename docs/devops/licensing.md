# Licensing Overview

This document explains how Corely is licensed under an open-core model. It is a practical summary, not legal advice.

## TL;DR

- **Corely Community (this repo)** is licensed under the GNU Affero General Public License v3 (**AGPL-3.0-only**).
- **Enterprise Packs / EE** are **commercial** and distributed separately.
- **Corely name and logos** are protected by trademark and brand usage rules.

## What is AGPL-covered in this repo

- All source code in `apps/`, `services/`, `packages/`, and `scripts/` is AGPL unless a file or folder says otherwise.
- The full license text is in `LICENSE`.

## What is commercial

- Enterprise Packs (a separate distribution) are licensed under a **commercial license** and are **not included** in OSS builds by default.
- See `ee/README.md` and `ee/LICENSE-EE.md` for the commercial license placeholder and distribution notes.

## FAQ

**Can I self-host for my business?**
Yes. AGPL permits commercial use, including self-hosting, as long as you follow the license obligations.

**Can I modify Corely and run it as a SaaS?**
Yes. If you modify and offer it as a network service, AGPL requires you to offer the Corresponding Source to users of that service.

**Can I use the Corely name/logo for my fork?**
No. Forks must remove Corely branding and choose a new name, per `TRADEMARKS.md`.

**Where are Enterprise Packs and how do I get them?**
